Your resume, honestly reviewed.

ResumeIQ came from a really simple frustration: most resume feedback is vague, awkward, or so generic that it barely helps you edit the page. I wanted something that could be fast, specific, and human enough to actually move you to the next fix.

This page preserves the original product demo. If live analysis is configured, you can upload a PDF and get a real breakdown. If not, the sample report still shows how the product was designed to think.
